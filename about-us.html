Masjid Information

Athlone is a town on the River Shannon situated in County Westmeath in Ireland. Its population is 20,000+, and became doubled in the last 10 years.
The town hosts AIT (Athlone Institute of Technology), Department of Education, many pharmaceutical companies such as Elan, Tyco, Bioclin Labs, and Coviden etc, and Telecommunication companies like Ericsson. Few years ago we registered a society named as ICSA (Islamic Cultural Society Of Athlone) with an aim to setup a cultural centre and a mosque within Athlone. Our charity number is CHY 19671 and we are registered with Revenue Commissioner of Ireland. For the last 10+ years we are praying at the basement of a shop which is in the main town centre. There are 5 daily prayers that are held in the masjid.
